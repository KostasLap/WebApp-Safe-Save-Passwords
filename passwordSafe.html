Welcome To The Safe World

A way to keep your passwords safe.

Instructions:

  1. Insert your username and password. Make sure you wrote them correctly!
  2. Click on the "Save Password" button.
  3. A file will be automatically downloaded that will contain your code completely different.
  4. To retrieve your original password just choose your file and press click on the "Reverse Password" button.
